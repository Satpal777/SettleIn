# Seeker (Client) Workflow

![SettleIn Logo](./images/flow%20(1).png)

The Seeker workflow covers the entire journey from property discovery to moving into a new home.

## 1. Discovery & Interest

![Browse Properties](./images/flow%20(20).png)

*   **Search**: Browse properties using filters (Budget, Move-in Date, City).
*   **Shortlist**: Save interesting properties to the shortlist for comparison.
*   **Visit Request**: Select a preferred date and time to visit a property. The landlord is notified to approve or suggest a change.

![Visit Details](./images/flow%20(12).png)

## 2. Visit & Evaluation

![Seeker Dashboard](./images/flow%20(18).png)

*   **Status Tracking**: Monitor visit status (Requested → Scheduled → Completed) on the Seeker Dashboard.
*   **Deciding**: After the visit, if the seeker wants to move in, the landlord initiates the move-in process.

## 3. Move-in Process (Resident Onboarding)
The seeker transitions to "Resident" status once the move-in process begins.
*   **Documents**: Upload Government ID, Proof of Employment, and Rental References.
*   **Lease Review**: Review the digital lease draft provided by the landlord.
*   **E-Sign**: Sign the digital agreement by typing their full name.
*   **Inventory Check**: Review and confirm the property inventory and condition.
*   **Finalization**: Once all steps are complete, the property is marked as "Rented" and the seeker is officially moved in.

## 4. Stay Management

![Resident Portal](./images/flow%20(5).png)

*   **Resident Portal**: Access a simplified dashboard for managing the stay.
*   **Extensions**: Request a stay extension by choosing a new move-out date and providing a reason.

![Extension Request](./images/flow%20(4).png)

*   **Tickets**: (Coming soon) Raise maintenance tickets for property issues.
